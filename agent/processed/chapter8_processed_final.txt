n. 谈判；协商
- [例]
  1. The negotiation process took several weeks. 谈判过程持续了几周。
  2. Both parties are optimistic about the outcome of the negotiation. 双方都对谈判结果持乐观态度。
  3. The negotiation failed due to lack of trust. 由于缺乏信任，谈判失败了。
- [搭] trade negotiation 贸易谈判；peace negotiation 和平谈判。
- [联] negoti(谈判) + -ation(名词后缀) ——> 谈判的过程。

contention /kənˈten.ʃn/
n. 争论；竞争
- [例]
  1. There was a fierce contention between the two teams. 两支队伍之间展开了激烈的竞争。
  2. The contention over the inheritance caused family discord. 继承权之争导致家庭不和。
  3. His main contention is that the law is unjust. 他的主要论点是法律不公平。
- [搭] political contention 政治斗争；intellectual contention 知识竞争。
- [联] con-(一起) + tend(拉紧) + -ion(名词后缀) ——> 拉紧在一起，产生冲突。

content /kənˈtent/
n. 内容；满足
- [例]
  1. The book contains a lot of useful content. 这本书包含了很多有用的内容。
  2. She seemed content with her life. 她似乎对自己的生活感到满足。
  3. The website provides fresh content every day. 这个网站每天提供新鲜内容。
- [搭] table of contents 目录；content creation 内容创作。
- [联] con-(一起) + tent(触手) ——> 所有东西放在一起。
